Our Services

What We Offer

At More Banquet Hall & Green Square, we provide a comprehensive range of services to make your event truly special. From venue rental to event planning, we've got you covered.

Hall Rental

Our elegant indoor banquet hall features crystal chandeliers, premium flooring, and state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems. Perfect for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations of all kinds.

  • Capacity for up to 1500 guests
  • Climate-controlled environment
  • Flexible seating arrangements
  • Premium sound system

Outdoor Lawn Booking

Our beautifully landscaped Green Square offers a serene outdoor setting with manicured lawns, decorative lighting, and a picturesque gazebo for ceremonies and outdoor receptions.

  • Capacity for up to 2500 guests
  • Scenic garden setting
  • Outdoor lighting options
  • Weather contingency plans

Custom Décor

Our talented décor team can transform our spaces to match your vision and theme. From elegant floral arrangements to custom lighting designs, we create the perfect ambiance for your event.

  • Customized theme designs
  • Floral arrangements
  • Lighting and draping
  • Table settings and centerpieces

Catering Services

Our culinary team offers exquisite dining experiences with customizable menus featuring a variety of cuisines. From elegant plated dinners to lavish buffets, we cater to all tastes and dietary requirements.

  • Multiple cuisine options
  • Customizable menus
  • Dietary accommodations
  • Professional service staff

DJ/Music Setup

Set the perfect mood for your event with our professional sound systems and DJ services. We offer a range of music options to keep your guests entertained throughout your celebration.

  • Professional DJ services
  • High-quality sound systems
  • Customized playlists
  • Lighting effects

Valet Parking

Provide your guests with a premium experience from the moment they arrive. Our professional valet service ensures a smooth and convenient parking experience for all attendees.

  • Professional valet staff
  • Organized parking management
  • Convenient guest drop-off
  • Available for all event sizes

Event Planning Support

Our experienced event coordinators work closely with you to ensure every detail of your event is perfect. From initial concept to execution, we provide guidance and support throughout the planning process.

  • Dedicated event coordinator
  • Vendor coordination
  • Timeline planning
  • Day-of coordination

Photography & Videography

Capture the special moments of your event with our professional photography and videography services. Our skilled team ensures that every important moment is beautifully documented.

  • Professional photographers
  • High-definition videography
  • Drone aerial footage
  • Photo editing and album creation
